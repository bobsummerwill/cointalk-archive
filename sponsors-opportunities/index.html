Sponsors & Opportunities

CoinTalk is free, uncensored and independent. Just like crypto-currencies, it’s powered by it’s audience!

Our audience is small but sincere and rapidly growing. Our current business model relies on tips and sponsorship to support CoinTalk.  What we can boast about however, is the fact that most of the people who listen to our show “get it”.  At this point our audience is a concerted bunch of early adopters, financial movers and shakers, as well as a wealth of entrepreneur strategists that are reinventing the economic and online realms. For what it’s worth, we also have a strong base of casual listeners that are looking for emerging technologies, trends, and solid insight into the nature of the crypto-currency domain.
