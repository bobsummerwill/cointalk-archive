CoinTalk 015 – Bitcoin Miami 1/5 – Debate Featuring Ethereum, Mastercoin and Bitshares

February 6, 2014BTC Miami 2014, Episodes, Ethereum, NewsKyle Kurbegovich

Today’s episode is a hybrid episode, CoinTalk and Let’s talk bitcoin headed to Sunny South beach Miami for the North American Bitcoin conference and got the opportunity to sit in on a 3 way panel organized by Let’s Talk Bitcoin – Guests on the panel include Ethereum’s Charles Hoskinson, MasterCoin’s David Johnston and Invictus Innovations Dan Larimer.

It turned out to be an exciting, topic rich and spirited debate – On top of this, Jason King from Sean’s outpost lead and moderated the panel, and he did a fantastic job as such. So CoinTalk is proud to bring you the video feed for what will surely go down in the record books as a spirited, powerhouse debate where all representatives had the opportunity to speak freely on new platforms, the likes of which could change the world forever.

February 6, 2014BTC Miami 2014, Ethereum, NewsKyle Kurbegovich

Vitalik Buterin takes the stage at a packed auditorium to talk about the benefits of Ethereum.

Ethereum travelled there anticipating many technical questions, as well as a philosophical discussion about the purpose of Ethereum. We were astounded by the overwhelming amount of interest and enthusiasm for our ideas. Vitalik’s presentation was met with both a standing ovation, and a question queue that took over an hour to address.
