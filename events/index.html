BITCOIN WEDNESDAYS AT BITCOIN DECENTRAL! – TORONTO, ON CANADA

Located at 64 Spadina Avenue, Toronto – 7PM Each Wednesday! Come join us!

Starting January 1st 2014, two themed monthly events will be added to the schedule in order to promote consistency, and to allow for more tailored and specific topic meetups.

We will now meet each Wednesday of the month with the following alternating theme schedule:

Week 1 – Bitcoin Social – Networking & News
Week 2 – Bitcoin 101 – What is Bitcoin?
Week 3 – Bitcoin Social – Networking & News
Week 4 – Bitcoin Entrepreneurs and Merchants Roundtable

As always all meetups are free to attend and open to all members, however, we will attempt to stay as true to theme as possible.

Check out: http://www.meetup.com/Bitcoin-Toronto/ for more information!
