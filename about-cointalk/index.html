About CoinTalk

CoinTalk is proudly Canadian!

On December 1st, 2013 CoinTalk launched the worlds first HD crypto-currency video show and podcast.  We are an independently owned and operated show founded by Kyle Kurbegovich and we record in the heart of Canada’s busiest metropolitan mega-city; Toronto, Ontario.

We are a salon style interview show with a focus for all things ‘crypto coin’.  Currently the only host is Kyle Kurbegovich, but if you are interested in doing correspondence or host-work; get in touch with us here to tell us how you want to help!

Our episodes are recorded twice weekly, with new episodes being released on Monday each week. Tune in as we navigate the ever changing landscape of new digital crypto currencies and provide a foundation of resources, guests and inside information to keep you up to date!

CoinTalk is a work in progress. It’s still the early days. 

So while we don’t know exactly what we plan to evolve into – we want to take the time to thank you for stopping by and tuning in! We’re proud to be a part of the movement, and we’re just as passionate and excited as you for things to come!
