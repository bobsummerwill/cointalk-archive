Bitcoin Decentral Launches!

December 31, 2013Bitcoin Decentral, NewsKyle Kurbegovich

New Years Day, Jan 1st 2014, 7pm – We want to see you at the party!

Bitcoin Decentral is a 5500 square foot 4 floor Coworking space in the heart of downtown Toronto. It will provide space for the Bitcoin Alliance of Canada, KryptoKit, Cointalk.ca, Etherium, The Toronto Bitcoin Meetup, The Bitcoin Expo 2014: In Cryptography We Trust, and other disruptive technology innovation initiatives and businesses that desire to be immersed in a space with people of like-minds dedicated to Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies.

If you’re in Toronto on January 1st, please drop by between 7pm and 10pm and witness the unveiling of Toronto’s first ATM and the first Bitcoin ATM made in Canada. Check here for details and location.

For more information visit BitcoinDecentral.ca
