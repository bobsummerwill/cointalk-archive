CoinTalk 016 – Bitcoin Miami 2/5 – Steve Dakh, Moe Levin & Margaux Avedisian

February 9, 2014BTC Miami 2014, Episodes, Kryptokit, News, UncategorizedKyle Kurbegovich

This episode is part 2 in our 5 part North American Bitcoin Conference coverage, we headed to south sunny South Beach Miami Florida to talk the the men and women behind the conference, the entrepreneurs involved in the crypto space, and some of the big names working to push the bitcoin revolution forward.

On today’s show:

Steve Dahk, the lead developer of Kyrptokit – the worlds fastest, easiest Bitcoin Wallet

Moe Levin from Triple Zero Media, one of the men responsible for the North American Bitcoin Conference

Margaux Avidesean from BitCapital and Lazerbee. 

CoinTalk is also proud to announce that tickets are now on Sale for Canada’s Bitcoin Expo which will be taking place in April, in the heart of Downtown Toronto, get your tickets and see the list of speakers at bitcoinexpo.ca – I’ll be there, and I look forward to meeting you in person!

For more information check out http://www.bitcoinexpo.ca

To everyone watching, if you’d like to help support CoinTalk, you can find our QR code at the end of the credits. It’s a blast being part of a motivated, exciting and growing community – and we have many new and exciting things coming for you in the future.
