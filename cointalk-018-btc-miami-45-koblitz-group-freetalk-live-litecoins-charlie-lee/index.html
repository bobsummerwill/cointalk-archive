CoinTalk 018 – Bitcoin Miami 4/5 – Koblitz Group, FreeTalk Live & Litecoin’s Charlie Lee

February 20, 2014Bitcoin Across America, BTC Miami 2014, CoinTalk Around the World, EpisodesKyle Kurbegovich

This episode is part 4 in our 5 part North American Bitcoin Conference coverage, we headed to south sunny South Beach Miami Florida to talk the the men and women behind the conference, the entrepreneurs involved in the crypto space, and some of the big names working to push the bitcoin revolution forward.

This episode is part 4 in our 5 part North American Bitcoin Conference coverage, we headed to south sunny South Beach Miami Florida to talk the the men and women behind the conference, the entrepreneurs involved in the crypto space, and some of the big names working to push the bitcoin revolution forward.

On today’s show we have:

Oliver Gale and Gabriel Abed – Koblitz Group
Mark Edge – FreeTalk Live and The Free State Project
Charlie Lee – Creator of Litecoin
